## TIPS FOR MAXIMUM CONVERSIONS

### Do This

- **Personalize the emails.** Replace placeholder sections with your real stories, opinions, and results.
- **Use your own voice.** Edit the tone to match how you normally write to your list.
- **Send at optimal times.** Morning sends (8-10 AM in your audience's timezone) typically perform best.
- **A/B test subject lines.** Each email has 3-5 options — test two per email if your ESP supports it.
- **Resend Email 7 to non-openers in the evening.** This is the single highest-converting send of the entire campaign. Dedicated evening-resend subject lines are included in Email 7 above.
- **Add your own bonus** to differentiate from other affiliates promoting the same offer.
- **Include the FTC disclosure** in every single email. This is not optional.

### Don't Do This

- Don't send all 7 emails on the same day.
- Don't change the core offer details (price, discount, features) — keep them accurate.
