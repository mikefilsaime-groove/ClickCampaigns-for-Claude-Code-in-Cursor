Reflecting on the beginning

That email was about affiliate marketing — selling other people's products for a commission. The idea seemed almost too simple. No inventory. No customer support. No shipping. Just promote a product, and if someone buys, you get paid.

Mike took the leap. Within 30 days, something remarkable happened: actual checks started arriving in his mailbox. Not huge amounts at first, but real money — earned while he slept. He called it "mailbox money," and it was the proof he needed that a different life was possible.

He didn't stop there. Mike dove headfirst into digital marketing, learning everything he could about online sales, funnels, and automation. He built and launched products. He created systems. He taught others what he knew.

Over the next two decades, Mike went from receiving small affiliate checks to generating tens of millions of dollars online. He built software platforms that have been used by millions of entrepreneurs around the world — names that became synonymous with online business itself.

Today, Mike is a millionaire who travels the world, works from wherever he chooses, and has created an ecosystem of tools that power thousands of businesses. But he never forgot where he started: broke, overworked, and one email away from a completely different life.

Jitendra isn't a theorist — he's a practitioner. He runs a successful SEO marketing agency and has personally built affiliate review sites that generate $700,000 per year in revenue.

When he met Mike, they realized the same playbook that made Jitendra hundreds of thousands of dollars could be automated with AI. What took Jitendra days to build by hand, AffiliatePages.com now accomplishes in just hours.
