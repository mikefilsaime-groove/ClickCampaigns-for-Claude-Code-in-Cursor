Our Story

I'm Mike Filsaime. Over the past two decades, I've created software used by millions of people—Groove.cm, Kartra, WebinarJam, EverWebinar, and Courses.gg.

I've been on both sides of this industry. I've built the tools, and I've used the tools. I know what works and what doesn't.

Scale Plus was born from a simple frustration: most "all-in-one" platforms aren't really all-in-one. They're missing pieces, charging for add-ons, or priced so high only enterprises can afford them. We set out to change that.
