Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, our live chat support is available Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm EST.

Do you offer phone support?

Phone support is available for annual plan members. Monthly members can access support through email and live chat. All users have access to our comprehensive help center.

Can I schedule a demo?

Yes! Select "Sales Inquiry" in the contact form above and mention that you'd like a demo. A member of our team will reach out to schedule a personalized walkthrough.

How do I report a bug?

Select "Technical Support" in the contact form and describe the issue in detail. Include screenshots if possible. You can also report bugs directly through the app using the feedback button.

Where can I find documentation?

Our Help Center has comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and step-by-step guides. Visit help.scaleplus.com or click the "Help" button inside your dashboard.
