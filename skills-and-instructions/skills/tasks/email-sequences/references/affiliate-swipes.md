# Affiliate Swipe Pack Reference Guide

## Overview

Affiliate swipe packs are ready-to-send promotional email templates designed for affiliates to use when promoting specific campaigns. They must be conversion-optimized while remaining easy for affiliates to personalize, maximizing affiliate revenue and maintaining brand consistency.

## Authority Foundation

Affiliate marketing principles from:
- **Mike Filsaime**: Swipe copy architecture and personalization placeholders
- **Frank Kern**: Relationship-based promotional approach
- **Gary Halbert**: Direct response persuasion fundamentals

---

## The Swipe Pack Structure

### Standard 3-Email Sequence

**Email 1**: Introduction & Value (Day 1)
- Personal recommendation from affiliate
- Initial value proposition
- Soft CTA to learn more

**Email 2**: Deep Dive & Benefits (Day 3-4)
- Detailed benefits and outcomes
- Social proof and testimonials
- Stronger CTA with urgency

**Email 3**: Urgency & Close (Day 5-7)
- Final value reinforcement
- Urgency and scarcity
- Maximum CTA urgency

### Extended 5-Email Sequence (For Launches)

**Email 1**: Announcement & Personal Story
**Email 2**: Value & Transformation
**Email 3**: Case Study & Social Proof
**Email 4**: Objection Handling
**Email 5**: Final Urgency & Close

## Critical Swipe Pack Elements

### 1. Personalization Placeholders

Make it EASY for affiliates to customize:

**Required Placeholders**:
- `{affiliate_name}` - Affiliate's full name or brand name
- `{affiliate_first_name}` - First name for conversational tone
- `{your_name}` - In signature blocks
- `{first_name}` - Subscriber's first name (their ESP merge tag)

**Optional Placeholders**:
- `{personal_story}` - Section for affiliate's personal experience
- `{why_i_recommend}` - Section for personal recommendation reason
- `{my_results}` - Space for affiliate's results (if applicable)
- `{bonus_offer}` - Affiliate-specific bonus they're adding

**Example**:
```
Hey {first_name},

It's {affiliate_first_name} here.

I wanted to share something I recently discovered that's been a game-changer for me...

{personal_story - Share your experience with this product/service, how you found it, and what it's done for you. 2-3 paragraphs recommended.}
```

---

### 2. Clear Instructions for Affiliates

Every swipe pack should include:

**Header Instructions**:
```
AFFILIATE SWIPE PACK: [Product Name]

CAMPAIGN DATES: [Start Date] - [End Date]
YOUR AFFILIATE LINK: [Insert your link here]

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Replace {affiliate_name} and {affiliate_first_name} with your name
2. Add your personal story in the {personal_story} section
3. Replace {your_name} in the signature
4. Update {affiliate_link} with your unique tracking link
5. Customize subject lines with your style (or use as-is)
6. Send emails 2-3 days apart for maximum impact

IMPORTANT: Make it personal! Share YOUR experience and why YOU believe in this offer.
```

---

### 3. Multiple Subject Line Options

Provide 3-5 subject line variations per email so affiliates can:
- A/B test
- Choose what fits their audience
- Match their typical email style

**Example**:
```
EMAIL 1 SUBJECT LINE OPTIONS:
1. "I found something you need to see"
2. "[Product Name]: This changed everything for me"
3. "{first_name}, quick question about [topic]..."
4. "The [outcome] solution I've been using"
5. "My honest review of [Product Name]"

Choose the one that fits your style or create your own!
```

## Complete Affiliate Swipe Templates

### Email 1: Personal Introduction & Value

**Subject Line Options**:
1. "I found something you need to see"
2. "[Product Name]: This changed everything"
3. "My honest recommendation for [outcome]"
4. "{first_name}, this is worth your time"
5. "Why I'm recommending [Product Name]"

```

I don't usually do this, but I need to tell you about something that's been a game-changer for me.

It's called [Product Name], and it's designed to help you [primary transformation].
```

## Extended 5-Email Sequence (For High-Ticket Launches)

### Email 4: Case Study & Transformation

**Subject**: "How [Name] used [Product] to [Result]"

**Focus**:
- Real customer story (provided by vendor)
- Before/after transformation
- Specific tactics they used
- Encourages reader to see themselves in the story

---

### Email 5: Objection Handling

**Subject**: "Answering your questions about [Product Name]"

**Focus**:
- Common objections (price, time, "will it work for me?")
- Address each with specific responses
- Reinforce guarantee and risk reversal
- Final CTA with urgency

## Swipe Pack Best Practices

### For Product Vendors Creating Swipe Packs

**Make It Easy to Personalize**:
- Clear placeholders
- Suggested customization areas
- Example personalizations shown

**Provide Multiple Options**:
- 3-5 subject line variations per email
- Optional sections (bonus offers, personal stories)
- Short and long email versions

**Include All Assets**:
- Banner images (with and without text)
- Product screenshots
- Testimonial graphics
- Social media graphics

**Set Clear Expectations**:
- Campaign dates
- Email cadence recommendations
- Key messaging points
- Compliance requirements

**Provide Support**:
- FAQ document for affiliates
- Swipe copy rationale (why it works)
- Affiliate manager contact info

---

### For Affiliates Using Swipe Packs

**Always Personalize**:
- Add your story and experience
- Explain why YOU recommend it
- Share your results (if applicable)
- Make it sound like you, not the vendor

**Match Your Voice**:
- Adjust tone to fit your brand
- Choose subject lines that match your style
- Modify language to sound authentic

**Add Value**:
- Include personal bonuses when possible
- Share your unique insights
- Offer to answer questions

**Test and Optimize**:
- A/B test subject lines
- Monitor open and click rates
- Adjust send times for your audience

**Follow Email Best Practices**:
- Mobile-optimize
- Keep paragraphs short (3-5 lines)
- Use clear CTAs
- Include PS sections

## Success Metrics

### Industry Benchmarks (Affiliate Emails)
- **Open Rate**: 20-35% (depends on list quality and relationship)
- **Click Rate**: 3-8% (promotional content)
- **Conversion Rate**: 1-5% (depends on offer, price point, and audience match)

### Affiliate Swipe Pack Quality Indicators
- **Personalization Rate**: 80%+ of affiliates customize (not copy/paste)
- **Affiliate Satisfaction**: Easy to use, clear instructions
- **Vendor Satisfaction**: Brand-consistent, high-converting

## Compliance & Legal

### Required Elements

**FTC Disclosure (USA)**:
```
[DISCLOSURE: This email contains affiliate links. If you purchase through my link, I may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. I only recommend products I personally use and believe in.]
```

**Placement**:
- Top of email (preferred)
- Before first affiliate link (minimum)
- In PS section (additional)

**Other Jurisdictions**:
- Check local affiliate marketing disclosure laws
- CAN-SPAM compliance (USA)
- GDPR considerations (EU)
- CASL compliance (Canada)
