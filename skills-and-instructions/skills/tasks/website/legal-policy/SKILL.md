# Legal & Policy Pages

Create clear, compliant legal pages (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Cookie Policy) that protect the business legally while remaining accessible and transparent to users.

## Core Objectives

- Ensure legal compliance (GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM, etc.)
- Protect business interests with clear terms
- Maintain transparency and user trust
- Balance legal protection with readability
- Reduce legal risk through proper disclosures

## Mandatory Elements

### 1. Privacy Policy
- **Data Collection:** What data is collected and how
- **Data Usage:** How collected data is used
- **Data Sharing:** Third-party sharing and partnerships
- **User Rights:** Access, deletion, opt-out rights (GDPR/CCPA)
- **Cookies:** Cookie usage and management options
- **Contact:** How to exercise rights or ask questions

### 2. Terms of Service
- **Acceptance:** What constitutes acceptance of terms
- **Service Description:** What the service/product provides
- **User Obligations:** What users must and must not do
- **Limitation of Liability:** Legal protections for the business
- **Dispute Resolution:** How disputes are handled
- **Changes:** How terms can be modified

### 3. Cookie Policy (if applicable)
- **Cookie Types:** Essential, analytics, marketing cookies
- **Purpose:** Why each cookie type is used
- **Management:** How users can control cookies
- **Third-Party Cookies:** External services using cookies

## Structure & Frameworks

### The "Clear & Compliant" Framework
1. **Plain Language:** Use simple terms where legally possible
2. **Organized Sections:** Clear headings and table of contents
3. **Legal Accuracy:** Ensure compliance with applicable laws
4. **User-Friendly:** Make it easy to find specific information

### Legal Page Structure
1. **Last Updated Date:** Prominent date of last revision
2. **Table of Contents:** Quick navigation to sections
3. **Main Content:** Organized sections with clear headings
4. **Contact Information:** How to reach for legal questions
5. **Acceptance Mechanism:** Checkbox or "I Agree" button (for ToS)

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

- **Professional & Clear:** Legal accuracy without unnecessary jargon
- **Transparent:** Honest about data practices and limitations
- **Accessible:** Use plain language where legally possible
- **Formatting:** Use clear headings, bullet points, and sections

## Concrete Examples

### Privacy Policy Data Collection Section
```text
"We collect the following types of information:

• **Personal Information:** Name, email address, phone number 
  (collected when you sign up or contact us)
• **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent, clicks (collected 
  via cookies and analytics tools)
• **Payment Information:** Processed securely through [Payment 
  Processor] (we do not store full card details)"
```

### Terms of Service User Obligations
```text
"By using our service, you agree to:

• Provide accurate information when creating an account
• Not share your account credentials with others
• Not use the service for illegal or unauthorized purposes
• Respect intellectual property rights of others"
```

## Quality Checklist

For every legal page, ask:
- [ ] Is it compliant with applicable laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)?
- [ ] Are data practices clearly and honestly explained?
- [ ] Is the language as clear as legally possible?
- [ ] Is there a clear "last updated" date?
- [ ] Would a user understand their rights and obligations?
