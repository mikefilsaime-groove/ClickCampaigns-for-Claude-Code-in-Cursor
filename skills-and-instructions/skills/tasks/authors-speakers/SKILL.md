# Authors & Speakers Marketing

Create strategic author and speaker marketing assets that position authors and speakers as authorities, build their platforms, and drive book sales and speaking engagements.

## Core Objectives

- Position authors and speakers as authorities
- Build professional brand presence
- Drive book sales and speaking engagements
- Create compelling marketing assets
- Maximize opportunities and revenue

## Speaker Kit Components

### One-Sheet (Speaker Sheet)

**Essential Elements:**
- Professional headshot
- Compelling headline/tagline
- Brief bio (2-3 paragraphs)
- Speaking topics (3-5)
- Key credentials
- Testimonials (2-3)
- Contact information

**Design Requirements:**
- Professional layout
- Brand colors
- Easy to read
- Print-ready (PDF)
- Digital-friendly

### Speaker Bio

**Structure:**
- Hook (attention-grabbing opener)
- Credentials and expertise
- Results and impact
- Personal touch
- Speaking experience
- Call to action

**Versions:**
- Long form (500+ words)
- Medium (150-200 words)
- Short (50-75 words)
- Tweet-length (280 chars)

### Speaking Topics

**Per Topic:**
- Compelling title
- Description (100-150 words)
- Key takeaways (3-5 bullets)
- Target audience
- Talk length options

### Additional Speaker Assets

**Speaker Reel:**
- 2-3 minute compilation
- Best speaking moments
- Audience reactions
- Stage presence showcase

**Media Kit:**
- High-resolution photos
- Logo files
- Press coverage
- Interview topics

**Testimonials:**
- Event organizer quotes
- Audience feedback
- Video testimonials

## Copywriting Style

- Professional and authoritative
- Opportunity-focused
- Achievement-highlighting
- Results-driven
- Compelling and memorable

## Quality Standards

- Every asset positions authority
- Every element is professional
- Every component drives opportunities
- No generic positioning
- Everything earns attention

## Quality Checklist

- Do assets position authority effectively?
- Are materials professional and compelling?
- Will this drive book sales and speaking engagements?
- Does this maximize opportunities?
- Will this build long-term authority?
