# Professional Services & Local Business Marketing

Create strategic professional services and local business marketing that drives local leads, converts prospects into clients, and maximizes local market presence.

## Core Objectives

- Maximize local visibility and SEO
- Drive qualified local leads
- Convert prospects into clients
- Build local authority and trust
- Maximize local market revenue

## Local SEO Strategy

### Google Business Profile

**Optimization:**
- Complete business information
- Accurate categories
- Service area definition
- Business description (keywords)
- Regular posts
- Q&A management

**Photos:**
- Exterior and interior
- Team photos
- Service photos
- Before/after (if applicable)

### Local Citations

**Key Directories:**
- Google Business Profile
- Yelp
- Facebook
- Industry directories
- Local chambers/associations

**Consistency:**
- NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
- Business hours
- Website URL
- Categories

### Local Content

**Page Types:**
- Service area pages
- City/neighborhood pages
- Local case studies
- Community involvement

**Keyword Strategy:**
- Service + City
- Service + "near me"
- Service + neighborhood
- Problem + location

### Review Management

**Generation:**
- Ask satisfied clients
- Email requests post-service
- In-person requests
- Easy review links

**Response:**
- Thank positive reviews
- Address negative professionally
- Show you care
- Invite back

## Service Marketing

### Service Pages

**Structure:**
- Service headline
- Problem/solution
- Benefits (not just features)
- Process overview
- Social proof
- Clear CTA

**Per Service:**
- Dedicated page
- Specific keywords
- Targeted content
- Local relevance

### Service Descriptions

**Framework:**
- Pain point acknowledgment
- Solution introduction
- Process explanation
- Outcome promise
- Trust building
- Call to action

### Pricing

**Options:**
- Starting at prices
- Package options
- Custom quote CTA
- Value justification

### Booking

**Elements:**
- Online scheduling
- Phone option
- Form inquiry
- Response time promise

## Lead Generation

### Contact Pages

**Elements:**
- Multiple contact methods
- Form with key fields
- Phone prominently displayed
- Hours of operation
- Response time expectation

### Landing Pages

**Structure:**
- Local headline
- Service focus
- Trust indicators
- Clear CTA
- Phone and form

### Call Tracking

**Setup:**
- Unique tracking numbers
- Source attribution
- Call recording (if appropriate)
- Quality scoring

## Email/SMS Follow-Up

### Lead Follow-Up

**Email 1 (Immediate):**
- Thank you
- Next steps
- Response time expectation

**Email 2 (24 hours):**
- Value reminder
- Service information
- Consultation offer

**Follow-Up Call:**
- Within 24 hours
- Personalized approach
- Schedule next step

### Post-Service

**Email 1 (Same day):**
- Thank you
- Service summary
- Support resources

**Email 2 (7 days):**
- Check-in
- Review request
- Referral ask

## Copywriting Style

- Local-focused, service-oriented, trust-building
- Emphasize local presence and expertise
- Use specific service benefits and outcomes
- Build trust through local authority
- Create desire through service value

## Quality Checklist

- Does this maximize local visibility?
- Are service pages conversion-optimized?
- Will this drive qualified local leads?
- Does this build local authority?
- Will this maximize local market revenue?
