# Influencer & Creator Marketing

Create strategic influencer and creator marketing campaigns that leverage creator partnerships to build brand awareness, drive engagement, and generate authentic results.

## Core Objectives

- Build authentic creator partnerships
- Create engaging creator content
- Drive brand awareness and engagement
- Maximize creator campaign performance
- Build long-term creator relationships

## Creator Campaign Strategy

### Creator Identification

**Target Creators:**
- Audience demographic alignment
- Content style fit
- Engagement quality
- Brand values alignment
- Past partnership performance

**Creator Tiers:**
- Macro (100K+): Broad reach
- Micro (10K-100K): Engaged communities
- Nano (1K-10K): Highly authentic
- UGC Creators: Content production focus

**Platform Focus:**
- Instagram
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Twitter/X
- LinkedIn

### Partnership Types

**Sponsored Content:**
- One-off posts
- Series or campaigns
- Branded content

**Affiliate Partnerships:**
- Commission-based
- Unique discount codes
- Trackable links

**Brand Ambassadors:**
- Long-term relationships
- Multiple content pieces
- Exclusivity agreements

**UGC Campaigns:**
- Content for brand use
- Ad creative production
- Social proof generation

## Creator Brief Components

### Campaign Overview

- Brand introduction
- Campaign goals
- Target audience
- Key messaging
- Timeline

### Content Requirements

**Format Specifications:**
- Platform(s)
- Content type (post, story, reel, video)
- Duration requirements
- Aspect ratios

**Creative Direction:**
- Key talking points
- Do's and don'ts
- Visual guidelines
- Brand assets to include

**Deliverables:**
- Number of pieces
- Raw content requirements
- Usage rights
- Revision process

### Guidelines

**Brand Voice:**
- Tone and style
- Key phrases
- Things to avoid

**FTC Compliance:**
- Disclosure requirements
- #ad and #sponsored
- Placement guidelines

### Compensation

- Payment amount
- Payment terms
- Performance bonuses (if any)
- Product/gift value

## Campaign Execution

### Outreach Process

1. Identify target creators
2. Research their content
3. Personalize outreach
4. Negotiate terms
5. Send contract and brief
6. Review and approve content
7. Track performance

### Content Review

- Brand alignment check
- Messaging accuracy
- Visual quality
- Disclosure compliance
- Technical requirements

### Performance Tracking

**Metrics:**
- Reach and impressions
- Engagement rate
- Click-through rate
- Conversions
- Cost per engagement

## Copywriting Style

- Authentic and creator-friendly
- Clear guidelines without restriction
- Collaborative tone
- Value-focused
- Excitement about partnership

## Quality Standards

- Briefs are clear and inspiring
- Partnerships are strategic
- Content resonates with audiences
- Campaigns drive results
- Relationships are long-term

## Quality Checklist

- Are creator briefs clear and inspiring?
- Are partnerships strategic and authentic?
- Will content resonate with audiences?
- Does this maximize campaign performance?
- Will this build long-term relationships?
