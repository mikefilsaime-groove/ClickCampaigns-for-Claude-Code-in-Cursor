# PR & Influencer Outreach

Create strategic PR and influencer outreach campaigns that build brand awareness, establish authority, and drive results through media coverage and influencer partnerships.

## Core Objectives

- Build brand awareness through media coverage
- Establish authority through PR placements
- Drive results through influencer partnerships
- Create strategic media relationships
- Maximize PR and influencer ROI

## PR Campaign Components

### Media Strategy

**Target Media:**
- Relevant publications
- Industry outlets
- General interest media
- Podcasts and broadcasts

**Media List Building:**
- Journalist research
- Editor contacts
- Beat alignment
- Past coverage review

**Story Angles:**
- Newsworthy hooks
- Timely angles
- Trend connections
- Expert commentary

### Press Materials

**Press Release:**
- Newsworthy headline
- Inverted pyramid structure
- Key facts and quotes
- Company boilerplate
- Contact information

**Media Kit:**
- Company overview
- Founder/spokesperson bios
- High-resolution images
- Brand assets
- Fact sheet

**Pitch Emails:**
- Personalized subject lines
- Hook in first sentence
- Relevant angle for outlet
- Clear ask
- Easy follow-up

### Outreach Process

1. Research journalist and outlet
2. Identify relevant angle
3. Personalize pitch
4. Send at optimal time
5. Follow up strategically
6. Track and respond

## Influencer Program Components

### Influencer Strategy

**Target Identification:**
- Audience alignment
- Content quality
- Engagement rates
- Brand fit

**Influencer Tiers:**
- Macro (100K+): Brand awareness
- Micro (10K-100K): Engaged communities
- Nano (1K-10K): Highly targeted

**Partnership Types:**
- Sponsored content
- Affiliate partnerships
- Brand ambassadors
- Product gifting
- Event appearances

### Influencer Outreach

**Initial Contact:**
- Personalized message
- Why them specifically
- Partnership overview
- Value proposition

**Partnership Proposal:**
- Campaign details
- Deliverables
- Compensation
- Timeline
- Creative freedom

**Contract Elements:**
- Content requirements
- Usage rights
- FTC compliance
- Payment terms
- Exclusivity (if any)

### Campaign Management

**Content Guidelines:**
- Brand voice alignment
- Key messaging
- Visual requirements
- Hashtags and mentions
- Disclosure requirements

**Performance Tracking:**
- Engagement metrics
- Reach and impressions
- Conversions
- ROI calculation

## Copywriting Style

- Professional and strategic
- Personalized, not templated
- Value-focused
- Relationship-building
- Clear and concise

## Quality Standards

- Every pitch compelling and personalized
- Every angle newsworthy
- Every partnership strategic
- No generic outreach
- Every communication earns attention

## Quality Checklist

- Are pitches compelling and personalized?
- Are story angles newsworthy?
- Are influencer partnerships strategic?
- Will this build authority?
- Does this maximize ROI?
