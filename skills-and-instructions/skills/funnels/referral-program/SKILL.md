# Referral Program Funnel

Build referral program funnels that incentivize existing customers to refer new customers, creating a sustainable growth engine. Reward advocates while acquiring new customers cost-effectively.

## Core Objectives

- Motivate existing customers to refer
- Make referral process simple and rewarding
- Acquire new customers cost-effectively
- Build sustainable growth engine
- Maximize lifetime value through referrals

## Program Page Structure

### Above-the-Fold

- **Headline:** "Refer Friends, Earn Rewards" or "Share the Love, Get Rewarded"
- **Subheadline:** Clear value proposition
- **Reward Structure:** What they earn for referrals
- **Social Proof:** Success stories, rewards earned
- **CTA:** "Start Referring" or "Get Your Link"

### Program Details

**How It Works:**
1. Get your unique referral link
2. Share with friends
3. Friends sign up/purchase
4. You earn rewards

**Reward Structure:**
- Per-referral rewards
- Tier levels (if applicable)
- Double-sided rewards (referrer + referred)
- Bonus milestones

**Referral Tools:**
- Unique shareable link
- Email templates
- Social media assets
- Copy-to-clipboard functionality

### Tracking & Dashboard

- Referrals sent
- Conversions
- Rewards earned
- Reward status

### Program Terms

- Clear eligibility rules
- Reward conditions
- Limitations
- Fraud prevention

## Reward Structures

### Types of Rewards

**Cash/Credit:**
- Account credit
- Cash payouts
- Discounts

**Product/Service:**
- Free products
- Extended subscriptions
- Premium upgrades

**Tiered Rewards:**
- Bronze: 1-5 referrals
- Silver: 6-15 referrals
- Gold: 16+ referrals

### Double-Sided Incentives

- Referrer gets X
- New customer gets Y
- Both benefit

## Email Sequences

### Invitation to Program

- Introduction to referral program
- How it works
- Reward highlight
- Get started CTA

### Referral Activity

- "Someone used your link!"
- Conversion notification
- Reward earned notification
- Progress to next tier

### Re-engagement

- Reminder about unused credits
- New referral milestone opportunity
- Limited-time bonus offer

## Copywriting Style

- Simple, rewarding, motivating
- Emphasize benefits and rewards
- Use specific examples and success stories
- Build excitement about earning
- Make it feel easy and worthwhile

## Quality Checklist

- Are incentives compelling and valuable?
- Is referral process simple and frictionless?
- Does program motivate action?
- Are rewards delivered promptly?
- Will customers want to refer?
