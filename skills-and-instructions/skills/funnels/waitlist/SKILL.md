# Waitlist Funnel

Build waitlist funnels that create massive anticipation, capture qualified leads, and convert them when your product or service launches. Create FOMO and exclusivity while building a qualified audience.

## Core Objectives

- Build massive anticipation and excitement
- Capture qualified leads and build audience
- Create FOMO through exclusivity and scarcity
- Convert waitlist members at launch
- Maximize launch day revenue

## Waitlist Page Structure

### Above-the-Fold

- **Headline:** "Join the Waitlist for [Product/Service]"
- **Subheadline:** Transformation promise or exclusive access benefit
- **Value Proposition:** What they'll get and why it matters
- **Social Proof:** Waitlist count, early access benefits
- **CTA:** "Join the Waitlist" or "Reserve My Spot"
- **Email Capture:** Simple form (email + optional name)

### Anticipation Section

**What's Coming:**
- Product/service preview
- Key features or benefits
- Transformation promise
- Why it's different

**Exclusive Benefits:**
- Early access
- Founding member pricing
- Exclusive bonuses
- Limited spots

**Launch Timeline:**
- Expected launch date
- Countdown (if close)
- What happens next

### Social Proof

- Current waitlist count
- Endorsements
- Media mentions
- Early feedback

### Scarcity Elements

- Limited early access spots
- Founder pricing for first X members
- Exclusive bonuses for waitlist only
- Time-limited benefits

## Pre-Launch Nurture Sequence

### Engagement Emails

**Email 1: Welcome (Immediate)**
- Thank you for joining
- What to expect
- Exclusive preview

**Email 2: Behind-the-Scenes (Day 3)**
- Development progress
- Why we're building this
- Sneak peek

**Email 3: Exclusive Preview (Day 7)**
- Feature highlight
- Early look at results
- Build excitement

**Email 4: Social Proof (Day 10)**
- Early user feedback
- Testimonials
- Industry validation

**Email 5: Launch Countdown (Day 14)**
- Launch date announcement
- What's included
- Early access details

### Launch Sequence

**Email 1: "Launching Tomorrow"**
- Final reminder
- What's coming
- Prepare to act

**Email 2: "Early Access Now Open"**
- Exclusive early access
- Special pricing
- Limited time

**Email 3: "24 Hours Left for Early Access"**
- Urgency
- What you'll miss
- Final CTA

**Email 4: "It's Live!"**
- Public launch
- Waitlist benefits ending
- Last chance

## Confirmation Page

**After Signup:**
- Confirmation message
- What to expect
- Social sharing incentive
- Refer-a-friend option (optional)

## Copywriting Style

- Exciting, exclusive, anticipation-building
- Emphasize transformation and outcomes
- Use specific benefits and previews
- Build FOMO through scarcity
- Create excitement about launch

## Quality Checklist

- Does waitlist build genuine anticipation?
- Is exclusivity real and compelling?
- Does nurture sequence maintain excitement?
- Is launch sequence conversion-optimized?
- Will waitlist members convert at launch?
