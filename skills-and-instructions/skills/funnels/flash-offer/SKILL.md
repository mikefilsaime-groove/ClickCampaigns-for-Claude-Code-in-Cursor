# Flash Offer Funnel

Build flash offer funnels that create massive urgency through limited-time promotions, driving rapid conversions with time-sensitive pricing or bonuses. Maximize immediate revenue through strategic urgency.

## Core Objectives

- Create compelling limited-time offer
- Build massive urgency and excitement
- Drive rapid conversions
- Maximize revenue in short timeframe
- Create FOMO through real scarcity

## Funnel Structure

### Flash Offer Landing Page

**Above-the-Fold:**
- **Headline:** "Flash Sale: [Offer] Ends in [Time]"
- **Subheadline:** Value proposition with urgency
- **Countdown Timer:** Real-time countdown prominently displayed
- **Offer Details:** What's included and pricing
- **CTA:** "Claim Now Before It's Gone"

**Offer Stack:**
- Core product with flash pricing
- Regular price vs. flash price comparison
- Expiring bonus stack
- Value comparison (showing savings)
- Limited quantity indicator (if applicable)

**Urgency Elements:**
- Countdown timer (synced and real)
- "Only X left at this price"
- Bonus expiration messaging
- Cart closing deadline

**Social Proof:**
- Real-time purchase notifications
- Testimonials from previous buyers
- Number of claims during flash sale
- Success stories

### Email/SMS Sequence

**Launch Email:**
- Flash sale announcement
- What's included and pricing
- Deadline and urgency
- Clear CTA

**Reminder Emails:**
- Mid-sale reminder
- Bonus expiring soon
- Last hours warning
- Final hour push

**Post-Sale:**
- Thank you to buyers
- "You missed it" to non-buyers
- Next opportunity teaser

## Copywriting Style

- Urgent, exciting, conversion-focused
- Emphasize time sensitivity and value
- Use specific savings and benefits
- Build FOMO through scarcity
- Create excitement about the deal

## Urgency Best Practices

**Real Scarcity Only:**
- Never fake deadlines
- Real countdown timers
- Genuine limited quantities
- Actual bonus expirations

**Urgency Language:**
- "Ends in X hours"
- "Only X available"
- "This price disappears at midnight"
- "Bonus expires when timer hits zero"

## Quality Checklist

- Is the offer genuinely compelling?
- Does urgency feel real and exciting?
- Is countdown timer prominent and working?
- Are bonuses valuable and actually expiring?
- Will this drive rapid conversions?
- Is the savings/value clear?
