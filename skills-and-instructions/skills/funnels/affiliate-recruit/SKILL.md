# Affiliate Recruitment Funnel

Build affiliate recruitment funnels that attract and convert high-quality affiliates who will promote your products effectively. Position your affiliate program as valuable and rewarding while qualifying affiliates.

## Core Objectives

- Attract high-quality affiliates
- Communicate opportunity and value
- Qualify affiliates effectively
- Onboard affiliates successfully
- Maximize affiliate performance

## Recruitment Page Structure

### Above-the-Fold

- **Headline:** "Join Our Affiliate Program" or "Partner With Us"
- **Subheadline:** Earning opportunity and value proposition
- **Commission Highlight:** Key commission structure
- **Social Proof:** Top affiliate earnings, testimonials
- **CTA:** "Apply Now" or "Join Program"

### Opportunity Section

**Commission Structure:**
- Base commission rates
- Tier levels (if applicable)
- Recurring commissions
- Bonus opportunities
- Payment schedule

**Product Information:**
- What they're promoting
- Conversion rates
- Customer lifetime value
- Pricing and offers

**Earning Potential:**
- Average affiliate earnings
- Top affiliate results
- Income examples
- Success stories

### Support & Resources

**Marketing Materials:**
- Banner ads
- Email swipes
- Social media content
- Video assets
- Landing pages

**Training & Support:**
- Onboarding process
- Strategy training
- Promotional calendar
- Dedicated support

### Success Stories

**Top Affiliate Examples:**
- Earnings achieved
- Methods used
- Time to results
- Testimonials

## Application Process

### Application Form

**Basic Information:**
- Name and contact
- Website/platform
- Audience size
- Promotion methods

**Qualification Questions:**
- Experience level
- Current promotions
- Marketing approach
- Commitment level

### Approval Process

- Application review
- Approval/decline notification
- Onboarding sequence
- Access to affiliate portal

## Onboarding Sequence

**Email 1: Welcome**
- Congratulations on approval
- Getting started guide
- Affiliate portal access

**Email 2: Resources**
- Marketing material overview
- Best-performing assets
- Quick start guide

**Email 3: Strategy**
- Top promotion methods
- Success tips
- Support resources

**Email 4: First Commission**
- Tips for first sale
- Quick wins
- Encouragement

## Copywriting Style

- Professional, opportunity-focused, motivating
- Emphasize earning potential and support
- Use specific commission examples
- Build trust through social proof
- Create excitement about partnership

## Quality Checklist

- Is opportunity compelling and clear?
- Are commissions attractive and competitive?
- Is support structure strong?
- Does onboarding set affiliates up for success?
- Will quality affiliates want to join?
