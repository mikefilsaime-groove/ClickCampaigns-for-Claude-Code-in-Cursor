# Tripwire / Self-Liquidating Offer (SLO) Funnel

Build flash offer/tripwire campaigns that rapidly convert new leads into buyers with irresistible, low-friction offers ($7-$97). Acquire customers profitably or at breakeven while segmenting high-intent buyers for backend monetization.

## Campaign Objectives

- Create offer delivering dramatic quick win within 24-72 hours
- Engineer scarcity/urgency for immediate action
- Collect buyer data informing upsell/cross-sell paths
- Break even (or better) on ad spend via order bumps and OTOs

## Funnel Architecture

### Tripwire Landing Page

**Offer Positioning:**
- **Hook Statement:** Problem + unique mechanism
- **Product Summary:** Promise, contents, immediate payoff
- **Value Stack:** Price anchor (retail vs. flash price)
- **Scarcity Levers:** Timer, quantity, bonus expiration

**Landing Page Structure:**
- Headline (stopping power)
- Proof section (testimonials, screenshots)
- Offer reveal with value stack
- CTA with urgency
- FAQ and objection handling
- Risk reversal/guarantee

### Order Form

- Risk reversal messaging
- Guarantee prominently displayed
- Testimonials near checkout
- Order bump placement
- Trust badges and security

### Order Bump

- Complementary quick win
- Low-ticket add-on ($7-$27)
- Headline with benefit bullets
- One-click add to order
- Price with value anchor

### One-Time Offer (OTO) Page

- Higher-ticket bundle or premium
- Continuity offer or flagship upgrade
- Headline and story
- Offer stack with bonuses
- Strong CTA with urgency
- Downsell if declined

### Thank You Page

- Order confirmation
- Activation instructions
- Bonus unlock
- Community invite
- Next steps

## Promotional Sequence

**Email/SMS Campaign:**
- Teaser email building curiosity
- Launch email with offer details
- Last chance email with urgency

**Paid Ads Concepts:**
- Problem agitation hooks
- Cost/benefit comparison
- Solution preview angles

**Retargeting:**
- Cart abandon messaging
- Last chance reminders
- Bonus expiring urgency

## Key Funnel Elements

### Hook & Promise Frameworks

- "Get X in Y days"
- "Steal the exact system"
- "Done-for-you templates"
- Address biggest objection immediately

### Social Proof

- Micro-testimonials and screenshots
- Quick wins from beta users
- Velocity stats ("3,427 downloaded in 48 hours")

### Scarcity Mechanics

- Deadline funnel timers (hard countdown)
- Limited bonuses (1-on-1 call, community access)
- Quantity thresholds (only 200 units)

### Profit Maximizers

- Order bump (swipe file, advanced training)
- OTO (premium bundle, membership trial)
- Post-purchase downsell/cross-sell

## Automation & Tracking

**Tagging Logic:**
- Buyers vs. non-buyers
- Bump takers vs. non-takers
- OTO converters

**Post-Purchase Nurture:**
- Onboarding emails
- Upsell follow-up
- Review request

**KPIs to Track:**
- EPC (earnings per click)
- Order bump take rate
- Upsell CVR
- Day-7 LTV

## Guidelines

**Avoid Tripwire Pitfalls:**
- Never: Create fake scarcity (people can tell)
- Never: Over-deliver to the point of devaluing paid products
- Avoid: Complicated checkout flows
- Skip: Long sales pages for $7-$27 offers

**Pricing Psychology:**
- $7-$17: Impulse buy, minimal friction
- $27-$47: Still low-risk, higher perceived value
- $67-$97: Requires more social proof and copy
- Always anchor against higher "retail" value

**Irresistible Offer Formula:**
- Solve a specific, painful problem
- Deliver a quick win (usable in 24-72 hours)
- Stack tangible value (show $ amounts)
- Add urgency that's real and believable
- Make the price feel almost ridiculous

**Order Bump Optimization:**
- Position as a "no-brainer add-on"
- Keep under $27 for highest take rates
- Complement but don't duplicate main offer
- Use checkboxes, not separate buttons
- Expect 30-50% take rate with good copy

**OTO Strategy:**
- Price 3-10x the tripwire
- Give them the "how" if tripwire gave the "what"
- Include bonuses not available elsewhere
- Accept that 5-15% conversion is normal
- Always have a downsell if they decline

**Urgency That Converts:**
- Deadline funnels work (countdown per visitor)
- Limited bonuses expiring is believable
- "Cart closes at midnight" creates real action
- Never run fake deadlinesâ€”you'll lose trust

**Funnel Math:**
- Front-end should break even or small profit
- Order bump adds $5-$15 average per order
- OTO/downsell is where profit lives
- Track EPC (earnings per click) religiously

## Quality Checklist

- Is offer genuinely irresistible at this price?
- Does headline stop scroll and create desire?
- Is every funnel element optimized for conversion?
- Does upsell stack value perfectly?
- Will customers feel delighted after purchase?
- Does this bridge naturally to flagship offer?
