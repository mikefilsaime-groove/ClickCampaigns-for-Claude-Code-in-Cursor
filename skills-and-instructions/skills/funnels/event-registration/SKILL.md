# Event Registration Funnel

Build event registration funnels that maximize event signups, build anticipation, and ensure high show-up rates. Position events as valuable experiences that create excitement.

## Core Objectives

- Maximize event registrations
- Build anticipation and excitement
- Ensure high show-up rates
- Create valuable event experience
- Drive post-event engagement

## Registration Page Structure

### Above-the-Fold

- **Headline:** "[Event Name] - [Date/Time]"
- **Subheadline:** Value proposition and transformation promise
- **Event Details:** Date, time, location/format
- **Key Takeaways:** 3-5 things they'll learn or experience
- **Social Proof:** Past attendee testimonials
- **CTA:** "Register Now" or "Reserve My Spot"

### Event Details Section

**Agenda:**
- Schedule breakdown
- Key activities
- Timing and format

**Speakers/Presenters:**
- Bio and credentials
- Photos
- Expertise highlights

**Value Stack:**
- What they'll receive
- Materials or resources
- Access and bonuses

**Format Details:**
- In-person, virtual, or hybrid
- Location (if in-person)
- Platform (if virtual)
- Time zone information

### Logistics Section

**For In-Person:**
- Venue information
- Directions and parking
- Accommodation (if needed)
- What to bring

**For Virtual:**
- Platform access
- Technical requirements
- Replay availability

### FAQ Section

- What's included?
- Who is this for?
- Can I get a replay?
- What if I can't attend?
- Refund policy

## Reminder Sequence

### Email Sequence

**Confirmation (Immediate):**
- Registration confirmed
- Add to calendar links
- What to expect

**1 Week Before:**
- Reminder with excitement
- Preparation tips
- Agenda preview

**1 Day Before:**
- Final reminder
- Access links
- Last-minute details

**Day Of:**
- "Starting Soon" reminder
- Direct access link
- Quick value recap

## Post-Event Follow-Up

**For Attendees:**
- Thank you and replay (if applicable)
- Resources and materials
- Next steps or offers
- Feedback request

**For No-Shows:**
- "We missed you" email
- Replay access (if available)
- Future event invitation

## Copywriting Style

- Exciting, value-focused, conversion-oriented
- Emphasize transformation and outcomes
- Use specific benefits and takeaways
- Build anticipation through details
- Create urgency around registration

## Quality Checklist

- Is event positioned as valuable and exciting?
- Are details clear and compelling?
- Does reminder sequence ensure attendance?
- Will registrants feel event delivered value?
- Does this maximize registrations and show-up rates?
