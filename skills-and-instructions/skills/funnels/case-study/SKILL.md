# Case Study / Proof Funnel

Build case study funnels that showcase success stories, build credibility, and convert prospects by demonstrating real results. Use proof and social proof to drive conversions.

## Core Objectives

- Showcase compelling success stories
- Build credibility and trust
- Demonstrate specific results
- Convert prospects through proof
- Create desire to replicate results

## Case Study Structure

### Landing Page

**Above-the-Fold:**
- **Headline:** "[Client] Achieved [Result] in [Timeframe]"
- **Subheadline:** Transformation summary
- **Key Results:** Specific numbers and metrics (prominently displayed)
- **Client Image/Logo:** Visual proof element
- **CTA:** "See How They Did It" or "Get Similar Results"

### Case Study Components

**Client Background:**
- Who they are
- Industry/niche
- Starting situation
- Why they're relatable

**The Challenge:**
- Specific problem or obstacle
- Pain points experienced
- Previous attempts
- Stakes involved

**The Solution:**
- How you helped
- Your method or approach
- Implementation process
- Timeline

**The Results:**
- Specific outcomes with metrics
- Before vs. after comparison
- Quantifiable improvements
- Qualitative benefits

**Client Testimonial:**
- Direct quote (video if possible)
- Their words about the experience
- Recommendation

### Conversion Path

**CTA Options:**
- "Get Similar Results"
- "Schedule a Consultation"
- "Apply for [Program]"
- "Download the Full Case Study"

### Supporting Elements

**Multiple Case Studies:**
- Various industries/niches
- Different result types
- Range of starting points
- Shows breadth of success

**Summary Statistics:**
- Average results
- Number of clients served
- Success rate
- Combined impact

## Case Study Page Types

### Single Case Study Deep Dive

Full detailed story with comprehensive results

### Case Study Gallery

Multiple case studies showcased with filter options

### Before/After Comparison

Visual transformation focus

### Results Dashboard

Metrics and numbers focus

## Copywriting Style

- Proof-focused, credible, results-driven
- Emphasize specific outcomes and metrics
- Use real numbers and timelines
- Build trust through authenticity
- Create desire to replicate success

## Quality Checklist

- Are case studies compelling and credible?
- Are results specific and believable?
- Does presentation build trust?
- Will prospects want to replicate results?
- Does this convert through proof?
