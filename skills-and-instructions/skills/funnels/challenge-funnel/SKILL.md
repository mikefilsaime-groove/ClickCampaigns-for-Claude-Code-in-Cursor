# Challenge Funnel

Build multi-day challenge funnels that activate prospects, deliver quick wins, and build momentum toward core offers (courses, coaching, membership, SaaS). Create experiences that feel like premium bootcamps.

## Challenge Objectives

- Deliver transformation or noticeable progress in 5-7 days
- Build daily engagement through live sessions, community, accountability
- Create urgency to join flagship offer during/after challenge
- Capture testimonials, social proof, and user-generated content

## Challenge Framework

### Core Elements

**Theme & Promise:**
- Clear transformation promise ("Launch your webinar in 5 days")
- Specific outcome they'll achieve
- Tangible deliverable by end

**Ideal Participant:**
- Target audience profile
- Prerequisites (if any)
- Commitment level required

**Schedule:**
- Daily time commitment
- Live session times
- Delivery channels

**Monetization:**
- Free challenge with paid offer at end
- Paid VIP upgrade option
- Tiered access levels

## Daily Structure

### Day 0: Onboarding

- Welcome email and community invite
- Orientation video/content
- Homework: Share goals, complete profile
- Community introduction

### Day 1: Kickoff

- Live kickoff session
- Set expectations, share roadmap
- Teach core concept #1
- Assign actionable task
- Community prompt: "Share your Day 1 win"

### Days 2-4: Deep Dive

- Daily live sessions
- Implementation workshops
- Progress checkpoints
- Public recognition of standouts
- Soft CTA introduction (success stories, case studies)

### Day 5: Transformation + Pitch

- Celebrate wins
- Recap journey
- Deliver major value
- Flagship offer pitch
- Clear next steps, bonuses, deadline

### Days 6-7: Cart Open

- Hot seat coaching
- FAQ sessions
- VIP upgrade opportunities
- Urgency emails/SMS
- Bonus expiration reminders

### Post-Challenge

- Thank you + next steps (buyers and non-buyers)
- Testimonial collection
- Feedback survey
- Evergreen asset creation

## Community Engagement Plan

### Platform Selection

- Facebook Group
- Circle
- Slack
- Mighty Networks

### Engagement Elements

**Pre-Challenge:**
- Introduction prompts
- Mindset priming
- Homework/preparation

**During Challenge:**
- Daily engagement prompts
- Polls and questions
- Wins thread
- Accountability tags
- Leaderboards (optional)

### Moderator Responsibilities

- Welcome posts
- Tag participants
- Answer questions
- Highlight successes
- Maintain energy

## Conversion Campaign

### Pitch Timing

- Day 3-4: Soft mention
- Day 5: Main pitch
- Days 6-7: Cart open with urgency

### Offer Stack

- Core offer
- Challenge-only bonuses
- Payment plans
- Fast-action incentive
- Deadline

### Follow-Up Sequence

**For Buyers:**
- Congratulations + access
- Onboarding into main program
- Success path

**For Non-Buyers:**
- Thank you for participating
- Value recap
- Waitlist for next opportunity
- Nurture sequence

## Operations Checklist

**Tech Stack:**
- Streaming software
- Calendar invites
- Reminder automation
- Workbook hosting
- Community platform

**Team Roles:**
- Host
- Moderator
- Tech support
- Success concierge

**Contingencies:**
- Backup stream link
- Replay delivery plan
- Support escalation

## Quality Standards

- Every day must deliver genuine transformation
- Every engagement prompt must build community
- Every conversion moment must be perfectly timed
- Experience must feel premium, not rushed
- Participants must feel part of something special

## Metrics to Track

- Registrations
- Show-up rate
- Engagement rate
- Completion rate
- Conversion rate
- Testimonials captured

## Guidelines

**Avoid Challenge Pitfalls:**
- Never: Run a challenge without clear daily outcomes
- Never: Make Day 5 ALL pitch (deliver value first)
- Avoid: Technical difficulties during live sessions
- Skip: Passive challenges where no one engages

**Challenge Structure Best Practices:**
- 5 days is ideal (7 max, 3 minimum)
- Daily time commitment: 30-60 minutes
- One clear deliverable per day
- Live sessions create urgency and FOMO
- Community is where magic happens

**Registration Page Optimization:**
- Promise a specific tangible outcome
- Show the daily breakdown (people love schedules)
- Use countdown to start date
- Social proof: "Join 2,000+ entrepreneurs"
- Low-friction signup (name + email only)

**Engagement Maximizers:**
- Daily email reminders with clear CTAs
- Community prompts that encourage sharing
- Recognize and celebrate participants publicly
- Create friendly competition (leaderboards optional)
- Respond to comments‚Äîevery single one

**Pitch Day Strategy:**
- Day 5 should be 70% value, 30% pitch
- Pitch AFTER delivering the final transformation
- Challenge-only bonuses create urgency
- Give them 48-72 hours to decide
- Follow up on Days 6-7 for non-buyers

**Live Session Best Practices:**
- Start on time (respect their commitment)
- Have a backup streaming plan
- Record EVERYTHING (replays matter)
- Engage with chat during sessions
- End with clear homework and next step

**Conversion Optimization:**
- Soft mentions on Days 3-4 build awareness
- Case studies from past challenge graduates
- Stack bonuses that expire after challenge
- Payment plans reduce friction
- Fast-action bonus for Day 5 buyers
