# Ecommerce Catalog Funnel

Build ecommerce catalog funnels that showcase multiple products, drive browsing and discovery, and maximize average order value through strategic product presentation and cross-selling.

## Core Objectives

- Showcase products effectively
- Drive product discovery and browsing
- Maximize average order value
- Optimize for conversions
- Create seamless shopping experience

## Catalog Structure

### Catalog Landing Page

**Above-the-Fold:**
- **Headline:** "Shop [Category/Collection]"
- **Subheadline:** Value proposition or collection theme
- **Product Grid:** Featured products with images
- **Filters:** Category, price, features
- **Search:** Product search functionality

**Catalog Organization:**
- **Product Categories:** Clear organization
- **Featured Products:** Highlighted items
- **New Arrivals:** Latest products
- **Best Sellers:** Popular items
- **Sale Items:** Discounted products

### Category Pages

**Page Elements:**
- Category header and description
- Product grid with filters
- Sorting options (price, popularity, newest)
- Product count
- Pagination or infinite scroll

**Filter Options:**
- Price range
- Size/variant
- Color
- Features
- Rating

### Product Page Optimization

**Product Details:**
- **Images:** High-quality photos (multiple angles)
- **Title:** Clear and benefit-focused
- **Description:** Compelling product copy
- **Features:** Key benefits and specifications
- **Pricing:** Clear and prominent
- **Reviews:** Customer testimonials
- **Add to Cart:** Prominent CTA

**Variant Selection:**
- Size selector
- Color selector
- Quantity selector
- Clear availability status

### Cross-Sell Strategy

**Related Products:**
- "You May Also Like"
- Similar products
- Same category items

**Frequently Bought Together:**
- Bundle suggestions
- Complementary products
- Complete the look

**Cart Recommendations:**
- Threshold messaging ("Add $X for free shipping")
- Related add-ons
- Popular additions

### Checkout Optimization

**Cart Page:**
- Clear product summary
- Quantity adjustments
- Remove option
- Subtotal and shipping estimate
- Continue to checkout CTA

**Checkout Flow:**
- Guest checkout option
- Minimal form fields
- Progress indicators
- Trust badges
- Order summary

## Copywriting Style

- Product-focused, benefit-driven, conversion-oriented
- Emphasize features and benefits
- Use specific details and specifications
- Build trust through reviews
- Create desire through product presentation

## Quality Checklist

- Are products presented compellingly?
- Does catalog drive discovery?
- Are cross-sells strategic and relevant?
- Is shopping experience seamless?
- Will this maximize average order value?
