# Scale Plus Brand Knowledge Base

## Brand Story

### Before
Creating software was our passion, but it was costly. A good quality developer costs between $8,000 and $15,000 per month. We outsourced developers in the Philippines, India, or Eastern Europe for about 75% off — but got 75% less quality. The trade-off wasn't worth it. Our vision to create quality software fast ended up being poor quality software very slow.

### The Inciting Event
AI coding came around. With Mike Filsaime's programming background and partner Matt Zeralta's expertise, we can use AI coding to prototype applications very fast. We can do in hours what would take months and in days what would take a year — with quality software on top of that.

### Today
We are creating applications at the speed of thought. AI coding has allowed us to create applications in hours that would normally take months. Our developers now use AI to refactor code, enhance UI, and create new software that members need much faster.

## Business Motivation

We created Scale.gg+ (Scale Plus) because we felt the Groove platform was good but needed expansion. We were strong for digital marketers using self-checkout funnels, but weak for client services businesses — agencies, coaches, consultants, professionals (accountants, lawyers, etc.) who need appointment booking, phone/Zoom calls, custom pricing, proper invoicing, and CRM that works with actual email addresses.
