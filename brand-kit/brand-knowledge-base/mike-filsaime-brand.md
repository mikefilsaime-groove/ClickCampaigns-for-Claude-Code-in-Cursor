# Mike Filsaime — Brand Knowledge Base

## Who Is Mike Filsaime

Mike Filsaime is a bold visionary at the intersection of **direct response marketing**, **technology**, and **artificial intelligence**. He is positioning himself as the foremost AI marketing consultant and agentic engineering leader in the digital marketing space.

He is the architect merging the proven science of direct response with the power of AI agents — creating what he calls **agentic marketing**: autonomous AI systems that plan, execute, and optimize entire marketing campaigns.

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Attributes
- **Bold** — Makes declarative statements. Doesn't hedge.
- **Visionary** — Talks about where marketing is going, not where it's been
- **Authoritative** — Speaks from $175M in real results, not theory
- **Direct** — No corporate fluff. Says what needs to be said.
- **Energetic** — Excited about what's possible, infectious enthusiasm

### Tone Guidelines
- First person when appropriate ("I've spent 20 years building the tools marketers use. Now I'm building the agents that replace the tools.")
- Future-focused framing. Legacy is proof, not identity.
- Technical credibility without jargon overload — he can go deep on AI but keeps it accessible
- Never self-deprecating. Never humble-bragging. Confident and matter-of-fact.

### Sample Voice Phrases
- "The marketers who win in 2026 aren't the ones using AI tools. They're the ones deploying AI agents."
- "I built WebinarJam, Kartra, and Groove. Now I'm building something that makes all of them look like calculators."
- "Direct response isn't dead. It just got an upgrade."
- "I don't teach AI. I build AI systems that do the marketing for you."

## Differentiators

1. **Track record + current innovation** — Not a newcomer to AI jumping on the trend. Not a legacy marketer stuck in the past. The rare person who's built $175M in marketing software AND is now building the AI layer on top.
